Basic Fundamental Human Rights

Right to Life and Liberty

The right to life and liberty is a fundamental human right, protected by various international treaties and national constitutions. It ensures that individuals have the freedom to live and pursue their goals without unwarranted interference or threat to their safety and well-being.

Right to Freedom of Expression

The right to freedom of expression guarantees individuals the freedom to express their opinions, thoughts, and ideas without censorship or restraint by the government or other authorities. It is essential for fostering democracy, promoting diversity of viewpoints, and advancing social progress.
